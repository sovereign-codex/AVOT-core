# Coherence Evaluation Rubric (v1)

The Guardian evaluates coherence with a 0.0–1.0 score based on:

## 1. Internal Logical Consistency (0–30%)
- No self-contradictions
- No circular reasoning

## 2. Alignment With SIOS-CORE (0–30%)
- Must not violate principles of safety and clarity

## 3. Multi-Agent Agreement (0–20%)
- Output should converge reasonably across agents
- Disagreements must be contextualized

## 4. Actionability & Clarity (0–20%)
- Clear next steps
- Low ambiguity
